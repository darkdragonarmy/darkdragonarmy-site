Specialized Units

Join our specialized divisions and find your calling within the realm. Each unit serves a crucial role in the Dark Dragon Army.

🎉 Council of Festivities

People of this unit host all DDA Events. Bring joy and celebration to the realm through epic gatherings and memorable occasions.

Directed by: Lady Kallista

⚔️ Royal Guards

People in this unit man the Walls of the Keep. Protect our realm and ensure the safety of all who dwell within our borders.

Directed by: Ser Thundershield

🏗️ Builder Guild

People in this unit are the builders of the DDA. Craft magnificent structures and shape the physical realm with your creative vision.

Directed by: Position Open

💰 Financial Unit

People in this unit pay members of the DDA. Manage the realm's treasury and ensure all loyal subjects receive their due compensation.

Directed by: The Founders

📚 Mentoring Guild

People in this unit guide & help members of the DDA. Share your wisdom and nurture the next generation of dragon warriors.

Directed by: Position Open

🌐 External Affairs

People in this unit handle all affairs outside the DDA. Represent our realm in diplomatic endeavors and forge alliances beyond our borders.

Directed by: Lady !Mami-Aly!

📋 Internal Affairs

People in this unit handle all affairs within the DDA. Maintain order and ensure smooth operations throughout our kingdom.

Directed by: The Founders

🔄 Transfer Office

People in this unit handle all transfers to the DDA. Welcome new members and facilitate their journey into our ranks.

Directed by: Position Open

🏒 HHL DDA Knights

People in this unit are part of the DDA team for the Habbo Hockey League. Compete with honor and bring glory to our name on the ice.

Directed by: Ser Djantonio
