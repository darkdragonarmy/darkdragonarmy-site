Builders Guild

The Builders Guild stands as the foundation of the Dark Dragon Army.

Tasked with constructing, designing, and maintaining all physical spaces of the Army, this guild ensures that every hall, battlefield, and chamber reflects the strength and identity of our kingdom.

Working in unison with both the Development and Creative Guilds, Builders transform ideas into reality — bringing life, structure, and permanence to the Army’s vision.
